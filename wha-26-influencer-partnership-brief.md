# WHA-26 Influencer Partnership Brief

## Campaign Overview

**Campaign:** "You're Not Alone in This"
**App:** What's Your Anxiety (WHA)
**Goal:** Drive authentic awareness, app downloads, and assessment completions through mental health creators who genuinely understand anxiety.

## What We're Looking For

We partner with creators who:
- Speak openly and authentically about their own anxiety or mental health journey
- Have an engaged, supportive community (not just high follower count)
- Create content that educates, validates, or provides practical help
- Align with our values: evidence-based, non-stigmatizing, hopeful but realistic

**NOT looking for:**
- Creators who romanticize or glamorize mental illness
- Anyone making medical claims or giving professional advice
- Overly polished/influencer-style content that feels inauthentic

## Creator Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Compensation | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| **Anchor** | 100K+ | $500-1,500 + affiliate | 2 videos, 3 stories, 1 post, affiliate code |
| **Mid-tier** | 25K-100K | $200-500 + affiliate | 1 video, 2 stories, 1 post, affiliate code |

## Content Guidelines

### What to Talk About
- Their personal relationship with anxiety (if comfortable)
- The anxiety assessment experience ("I took it and found out...")
- A specific WHA tool they use and why it helps
- Why understanding your anxiety type matters
- Normalizing anxiety and mental health conversations

### What NOT to Say
- "This cured my anxiety" (it's a tool, not a cure)
- "You should stop therapy and use this instead"
- Any medical claims or diagnostic language
- Guaranteed results or percentages about personal outcomes
- Comparing WHA to medication or professional treatment

### Required Disclosures
- #ad or #sponsored (or platform equivalent) for paid partnerships
- "Not a replacement for professional therapy" mention
- Honest, authentic tone — no scripted or overly polished delivery

### Content Formats (Creator's Choice)

**Option A: Assessment Reaction**
Creator takes the assessment on camera, shares results, and talks about how it felt to get their anxiety type identified.

**Option B: Tool Demo**
Creator shows a WHA tool they use regularly (breathing, grounding, SOS) and explains how it fits into their routine.

**Option C: Story Time**
Creator shares a personal anxiety story and mentions WHA as a tool that helped them understand or manage it.

**Option D: Educational**
Creator makes their usual educational content about anxiety and naturally incorporates WHA as a resource.

## Talking Points (Optional — Creators Should Use Their Own Voice)

- "There are different types of anxiety and most people don't know which one they have."
- "Knowing your anxiety type isn't about labeling yourself — it's about finding tools that actually work for YOUR type."
- "WHA is free to start and the assessment takes 2 minutes."
- "The SOS panic tool is always free, even without an account."
- "This isn't a replacement for therapy. It's a tool you use alongside whatever else helps you."

## Vetting Checklist

Before partnering, verify:
- [ ] Content aligns with our values (non-stigmatizing, evidence-aware)
- [ ] Engagement rate > 3% (not just follower count)
- [ ] No history of harmful mental health content or misinformation
- [ ] Audience demographic matches our target (18-45, wellness-interested)
- [ ] Brand safety: no controversial or polarizing content that conflicts
- [ ] Creator has disclosed past partnerships appropriately
