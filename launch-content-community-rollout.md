# WHA-15 Launch Content and Community Rollout

## Objective
Deliver a pre-launch and launch-week content + community operating plan that can be executed immediately and handed off to the future Content & Community Manager without rework.

## Audience and Channel Priorities
- Primary audience: technical operators, product owners, and growth leaders evaluating workflow tooling.
- Secondary audience: creators and practitioners who influence tooling decisions in teams.
- Priority channels: LinkedIn, X, newsletter, launch landing page/blog, and community spaces (Slack/Discord/Reddit).

## Messaging Pillars
- Ship faster with fewer handoffs.
- Maintain quality and governance while moving quickly.
- Show proof with concrete before/after outcomes.

## Pre-Launch Content Calendar (T-14 to T-1)

### Week 1 (T-14 to T-8)
- Long-form post: launch problem framing + market narrative.
- Social thread #1: 5 pain points and how launch addresses each.
- Teaser short video #1 (15-20s): "what is changing".
- Newsletter draft: early-access angle and launch date hold.
- Community prompt #1: collect top workflow bottlenecks from members.

### Week 2 (T-7 to T-1)
- Long-form post: product walkthrough + value proof.
- Social thread #2: customer-style use cases and outcomes.
- Teaser short video #2 (15-30s): launch-day CTA preview.
- Newsletter send: launch-week agenda and signup CTA.
- Community AMA announcement with date/time and question intake form.

## Launch-Week Content Calendar (T0 to T+7)

### Launch Day (T0)
- Publish launch article + landing page update.
- Publish founder/CMO launch post on LinkedIn and X.
- Publish short demo clip and pinned CTA post.
- Send launch email to subscriber list.
- Host live AMA (30-45 minutes) in community channel.

### Post-Launch Week (T+1 to T+7)
- Day 2: quick-start guide post + onboarding checklist.
- Day 3: customer scenario carousel (3 use cases).
- Day 4: objection-handling post (security, migration, effort).
- Day 5: creator spotlight/user-generated clip roundup.
- Day 6: recap thread with metrics/proof points.
- Day 7: last-call CTA for launch offer or priority onboarding.

## Community Engagement Plan
- Response SLA:
  - <4 business hours for product and onboarding questions.
  - <24 hours for feature requests and roadmap discussions.
- Moderation framework:
  - Tag questions by type: onboarding, bug, feature, pricing, integration.
  - Escalate bugs and blockers to product/engineering within same day.
- Community programming:
  - 1 live AMA during launch day.
  - 1 office-hours session during T+3 to T+5.
  - Weekly prompt cadence for first 4 weeks after launch.
- Engagement KPI targets:
  - Comment/reply rate >8% on launch posts.
  - Community post participation from at least 20 unique members in launch week.
  - At least 10 community-sourced UGC artifacts by T+14.

## Creator and Community Activation Plan
- Creator tiers:
  - Tier 1 (anchor): 3-5 niche creators with audience fit and strong trust.
  - Tier 2 (support): 10-15 micro-creators for amplification.
- Offer structure:
  - Early access + co-marketing visibility.
  - Performance-linked incentive (flat + qualified signup bonus).
- Activation workflow:
  - T-10: outreach and briefing docs sent.
  - T-7: creative alignment and key-message lock.
  - T-2: final asset and link QA.
  - T0 to T+3: coordinated posts go live.
  - T+7: performance review and renewal shortlist.

## Operating Assets Checklist
- [ ] Master content calendar with owners and due dates.
- [ ] Approved copy bank by channel and message pillar.
- [ ] Creative asset kit (statics, clips, thumbnails, captions).
- [ ] UTM matrix and tracking links for all external placements.
- [ ] Community moderation macros and escalation map.
- [ ] Creator brief, agreement terms, and reporting template.

## Metrics and Reporting Cadence
- Daily (launch week):
  - Reach, engagement, CTR, signups by channel.
  - Community active contributors and unanswered threads.
- Twice weekly (post-launch month 1):
  - Top-performing topics/formats.
  - Creator contribution to assisted conversions.
- Weekly executive readout:
  - Wins, misses, and next 7-day optimization plan.

## Ownership and Handoff
- Current owner: CMO (temporary).
- Future owner: Content & Community Manager (on hire approval).
- Handoff package includes:
  - this rollout plan,
  - launch-week publishing checklist,
  - creator contact sheet,
  - response/messaging playbook,
  - metrics dashboard links and KPI definitions.

## Dependencies and Risks
- Dependency: hiring chain in `WHA-12` for long-term execution ownership.
- Risk: delayed owner assignment can reduce response velocity after launch week.
- Mitigation: pre-build templates, macros, and creator workflows so transition is same-day.
