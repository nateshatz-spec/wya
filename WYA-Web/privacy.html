WYA 3.0

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

WYA Wellness ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the WYA 3.0 mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Mood & Journal Data: Entries you make regarding your emotional state, thoughts, and reflections.
  • Clinical Data: Information entered into CBT, DBT, and Anger Management labs.
  • Health Data: Medication logs, sleep data, and recovery tracking information.
  • Profile Information: Your name and email address if provided during setup.

2. How Your Data is Stored

By default, WYA 3.0 stores your data locally on your device. If you choose to enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted during transmission and at rest on our secure servers.

3. Use of Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide personalized "Aura Intelligence" insights and correlations.
  • Generate clinical reports for your personal use or sharing with medical professionals.
  • Improve the app's interactive tools and features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal health data to third parties. We only share your data with your explicit consent (e.g., when you use the "Clinical Export" feature to send a report to your doctor).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your data at any time through the app settings. Deleting the app will also remove all locally stored data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@wya-wellness.com.
