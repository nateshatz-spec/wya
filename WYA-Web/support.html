Frequently Asked Questions

How do I backup my data?

Data is stored locally on your device by default. You can enable Cloud Sync in the Settings menu to securely backup your data to our servers.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. Your health data is private. It is only shared if you explicitly choose to export a report for your healthcare provider.

What is "Aura Intelligence"?

Aura Intelligence is our proprietary engine that analyzes your mood logs and lifestyle factors to help you visualize emotional patterns.
